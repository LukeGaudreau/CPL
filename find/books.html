Books & E-Books

Books

Your library card is the key to the resources of Cambridge's Main Library and its six branches. It is also your membership into the Minuteman Library Network (MLN). All MLN library members allow you to borrow their materials on your Cambridge library card. The Cambridge Public Library honors all library cards issued by other MLN libraries.

E-Books

Overdrive is a newer collection of eBooks and Downloadable Audio Books that does support Apple formats. Overdrive has approximately 169 titles that can be played on a variety of mobile devices (depending on publisher permissions and device specifications), and more are being added every day.

NetLibrary has a huge database of eBooks with over 3,000 titles at your disposal. However, this service has been discontinued and nothing new is being added to the collection. It also does not support any Apple formats. You will need to create a free account with them to begin.
