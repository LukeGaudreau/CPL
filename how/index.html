How Do I?

Access online library resources from home?

Our Minuteman Library Network Catalog is accessible from home computers.

Book a meeting room?

The Cambridge Public Library has meeting room space for community organizations in its Main Library and the O'Neill Branch Library.

Donate materials?

We do accept some donations. Please check our policy.

Get a library card?

Library cards are free. Just go to one of the Cambridge public libraries with ID and proof of address.

Get a museum pass?

We offer passes and coupons to over a dozen museums and institutions in the area.

Get materials from other libraries?

You can reserve materials from other libraries in person or by using our on-line catalog.

Get materials delivered to my home?

Monthly delivery service is available for residents who are homebound.

Get online at the library?

All of the Cambridge Public Libraries have free wireless access and computers for internet access.

Renew library materials from home?

Patrons may renew library materials by phone or by using the on-line catalog.
