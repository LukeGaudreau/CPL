Meeting Rooms

Main Library

The Main Library has a number of meeting rooms available for use by Cambridge-based not-for-profit community groups.The meeting rooms and capacity are:


Beech Room: capacity 16

Rindge Room: capacity 12

Community Room: capacity 100

Lecture Hall: capacity 230

Fuller Room: capacity 6

Computer classroom: capacity 20

All meeting rooms are subject to availability with the understanding that library programs have priority. If interested in booking a meeting room, please fill out a meeting room application and submit this to the Main Library no more than two months in advance.You can also fax the application to (617)349-4028.

For complete information, please see our policy and application.You can also call the Library Administration Office at (617) 349-4032 with questions.

Main Library Meeting Rooms Policy and Application

Branch Libraries

Central Square Branch: Lewis Room and Rotary Conference Room are not available while the branch is closed.

The O'Neill Branch Meeting Room can be booked for two hours and can accomodate 6 - 8 people maximum. For more information, call (617) 349-4023.
