How do I book a meeting room?

Main Library

Meeting Rooms Policy and Application Form

The Main Library meeting rooms are available for use by Cambridge-based not-for-profit community groups.

  • Beech Room: capacity 16
  • Rindge Room: capacity 12
  • Community Room: capacity 100
  • Lecture Hall: capacity 230
  • Fuller Room: capacity 6
  • Computer classroom: capacity 20

All meeting rooms are subject to availability with the understanding that library programs have priority. If interested in booking a meeting room, please fill out a meeting room application and submit it to the Main Library no more than two months in advance.You can also fax the application to (617)349-4028.

Branch Libraries

The Central Square Branch Lewis Room and Rotary Conference Room are not available while the branch is closed.

The O'Neill Branch Meeting Room can be booked for two hours and can accomodate 6 - 8 people maximum. For more information, call (617) 349-4023.
