Closed on Veterans' Day

All library branches will be closed on Thursday November 11th in observance of Veterans' Day.

November 3, 2010

Central Square Branch closed

We regret to advise our users, but the Central Square Branch will be closed longer than anticipated due to renovations to the building's heating and cooling systems. We will use this opportunity to renovate the aging public restrooms and the basement Literacy Center. Upon completion, the branch will be more comfortable and attractive to better serve the neighborhood.

We apologize for the inconvencience this may cause, and direct our users to the other branches of the Cambridge Public Library. At this time we are unable to establish a re-opening date. We are making every effort to make efficient and expedient renovations and open the branch as soon as possible.

October 7, 2010

Main Library open Sundays

The Main Library is once again open 1-5pm on Sundays during the school year. Visit the branch locations page for a full listing of our hours.

September 1, 2010
